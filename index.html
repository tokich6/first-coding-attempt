Magical Fluffy Pancakes

You can't code on an empty stomach,
these magical pancakes will keep you full til your next pzza party.
Serves 4 humans or 1 unicorn.

Pancakes are objectively the greatest things in the world.
I can't live without them.
Harry Crank

Make them yourself

Ingredients

  • 200g of Unicorn Flour
  • 1 large donkey egg
  • pinch of sault
  • 125 milk
  • 2tsp rancid yak butter

Methods

  1. Add unicorn flour and salt to a bowl
  2. Add one egg into the centre of the bowl, pour in the milk slowly
  3. Add melted butter
  4. Mix together slowly until butter is smooth
  5. Add one ladel of butter to a hot magical skillet, flip after 2 minutes
  6. Stack and serve with butter, syrup and glitter
